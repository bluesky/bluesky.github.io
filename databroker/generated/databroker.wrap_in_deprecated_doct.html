databroker.wrap_in_deprecated_doct¶

databroker.wrap_in_deprecated_doct(name, doc)[source]¶

Put document contents into a DeprecatedDoct object.

See wrap_in_doct(). The difference between DeprecatedDoct and doct.Document is a warning that dot access (__getattr__() as a synonym for __getitem__()) may be removed in the future.
